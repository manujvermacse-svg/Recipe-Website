Margherita Pizza

Preparation Time: 40 minutes (with dough resting)

Difficulty: Easy

Category: Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 500g pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 200ml tomato sauce
  • 250g fresh mozzarella cheese (torn into pieces)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F) for at least 15 minutes until it reaches the correct temperature.
  2. Take pizza dough and stretch it gently on a lightly floured surface into a 10-inch round shape about 0.25 inches thick.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet, and prick it lightly with a fork to prevent air bubbles.
  4. Brush the dough lightly with olive oil on all edges to help them brown.
  5. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving about 0.5 inches border around the edges for the crust.
  6. Sprinkle dried oregano, salt, and a pinch of black pepper over the sauce.
  7. Distribute torn pieces of fresh mozzarella cheese evenly across the pizza, leaving some space between pieces.
  8. Drizzle remaining olive oil lightly over the cheese.
  9. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and slightly bubbly.
  10. Remove from oven and immediately place fresh basil leaves on top.
  11. Let cool for 2-3 minutes before slicing and serving.
