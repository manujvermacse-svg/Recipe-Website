Caesar Salad

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Category: Vegetarian

Ingredients

For the Salad:

  • 1 large head of romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup croutons (store-bought or homemade)
  • 0.5 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Caesar Dressing:

  • 0.5 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
  • 0.5 teaspoon black pepper
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • Water (2-3 tablespoons to adjust consistency)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Caesar dressing by combining mayonnaise, lemon juice, and Worcestershire sauce in a bowl.
  2. Add minced garlic, grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and black pepper to the dressing mixture.
  3. Mix all dressing ingredients thoroughly until smooth and well combined.
  4. If the dressing is too thick, add water one tablespoon at a time until you reach desired consistency.
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning with more salt, pepper, or lemon juice as needed.
  6. Remove the outer leaves from the romaine lettuce and rinse thoroughly under cold water.
  7. Dry the lettuce completely with paper towels or use a salad spinner to prevent excess water.
  8. Chop or tear the romaine lettuce into bite-sized pieces into a large salad bowl.
  9. Pour the Caesar dressing over the lettuce.
  10. Toss gently but thoroughly until all lettuce leaves are evenly coated with dressing.
  11. Add croutons just before serving to maintain their crispness.
  12. Top with additional grated Parmesan cheese and a pinch of black pepper.
  13. Serve immediately on chilled plates for best results.
