Chocolate Brownies

Preparation Time: 35 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Category: Dessert

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 200g dark chocolate (chopped)
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 0.5 cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 0.5 cup cocoa powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon baking powder
  • 0.25 teaspoon salt
  • 100g walnuts or chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175°C (350°F) and line a 9x9 inch baking pan with parchment paper or grease it well with butter.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine butter and chopped dark chocolate. Heat in 30-second intervals, stirring between each, until completely melted and smooth.
  3. Remove from microwave and stir in brown sugar and white sugar until well combined.
  4. Allow the mixture to cool slightly for 2-3 minutes until it's warm but not hot.
  5. Beat in eggs one at a time, stirring well after each addition to fully incorporate.
  6. Add vanilla extract and stir until evenly distributed.
  7. In a separate bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  8. Gently fold the dry ingredients into the chocolate mixture until just combined (do not overmix).
  9. Fold in walnuts or chocolate chips if using.
  10. Pour the batter into the prepared baking pan and smooth the top evenly.
  11. Bake for 20-25 minutes until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs (not wet).
  12. Remove from oven and allow to cool in the pan for 10-15 minutes.
  13. Turn out onto a wire rack or leave in the pan, then cut into squares once completely cooled.
  14. Store in an airtight container for up to 5 days.
